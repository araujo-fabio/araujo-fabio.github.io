Article: About Me

Written by Fabio H. Araujo

My name is Fabio, and I'm a 17 year old lurker from Sao Paulo, Brazil.

Lifestyle

I don't have a favorite song, food, band or anything for much longer. My tastes don't work that way: I can't judge whether I like The Offspring or Korn better, because I like them in different ways. My mood sometimes will be more in favour of something, but I know that this is going to end sometime.
But I can say what I like most of the time, and this is what I'm going to write here.

Music

Genres I often enjoy are mostly rockish. I'm into Alternative Rock, New Metal, Punk Rock, Deathcore, almost anything rock-based. You can see what I'm hearing in the past days visiting my last.fm page (see Links in the end of this article).
Linkin Park may be my favorite band of all time: been a fan since a kid, maybe since I was born (I've got pictures with LP t-shirts when I was 1 year old). For me, the band will never get boring, along with side-projects like Fort Minor and Dead by Sunrise.

Videogames

I'm not into videogames, especially newer ones because they look so boring, slow-paced and too much cinematic. I also miss the soul developers put into games - something that was lost when the game industry became so big and artificial. I tend to like games with strategic thinking, or anything with great aesthetics overall.
My favorite videogames are: Tetris, old-school FPS (Doom, Blood and Duke Nukem 3D), Street Fighter, DotA2, Counter-Strike 1.6, Halo (Bungie era), Starcraft.
I also own a Steam account for some PC games, and a GameFAQs account so I can track anything I own physically. I most pirate or emulate games, but you can find links to my accounts in the end of this article anyway.

Politics, philosophy and ativism

I'm a anarcho-capitalist (or a Libertarian by Brazilian standards, which is nowhere closer to the American standards). I'm also a cypherpunk , and I'm always learning new ways to free software and ensure privacy.

Reading

Most of the things I read are relationed to either anarcho-capitalism or cypherpunk topics. Besides that, I enjoy reading Absurdism and Symbolism.

How I do my computing

I use a desktop with some OK specs. I use most than one Linux distro. My main one is currently using Ubuntu (a Debian based distro), but I also have a HDD with Windows installed on it for some purposes. My favorite graphical interface is XFCE. I'm looking foward to buying a Thinkpad.
I use a Redmi Note 4X phone with a custom Android ROM installed on it.
I mostly use my computer to storage things such as documents and music. I also use it for some programs that are required in work or studies.
